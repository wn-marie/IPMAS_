# FAOSTAT Food Security Data

Place your FAOSTAT datasets in this directory.

## üìä Expected Files

### Food Security Indicators
- Format: CSV, Excel, JSON
- Naming: `FAOSTAT_FOOD_SECURITY_YYYY.*`
- Contains: Food insecurity prevalence, dietary energy supply

### Agricultural Production
- Format: CSV, Excel
- Naming: `FAOSTAT_PRODUCTION_YYYY.*`
- Contains: Crop production, yields, area harvested

### Food Prices
- Format: CSV, Excel
- Naming: `FAOSTAT_PRICES_YYYY.*`
- Contains: Food price indices, commodity prices

## üì• How to Download

1. Visit https://www.fao.org/faostat/en/
2. Navigate to "Data" ‚Üí "Food Security and Nutrition"
3. Select Kenya as country
4. Choose indicators
5. Export to CSV or Excel

## üîç Key Indicators

- Prevalence of undernourishment
- Prevalence of moderate/severe food insecurity
- Dietary energy supply adequacy
- Share of dietary energy from cereals/roots/tubers
- Food price indices
